Operating Systems - Interview Prep

1. Process vs Thread

Process: independent execution unit. Thread: lightweight, shares memory.

2. Scheduling Algorithms

FCFS, SJF, Round Robin, Priority Scheduling. Metrics: turnaround, waiting time.

3. Deadlocks

Deadlock conditions: mutual exclusion, hold & wait, no preemption, circular wait. Avoid with Banker's Algorithm.

4. Memory Management

Paging, segmentation, virtual memory, demand paging, page replacement (LRU, FIFO).
