DBMS - Interview Prep

1. Normalization

Reduces redundancy by organizing data. Forms: 1NF, 2NF, 3NF, BCNF.

2. Transactions & ACID

Transactions ensure atomic operations. ACID: Atomicity, Consistency, Isolation, Durability.

3. Indexing

Speeds up searches. B+ Trees commonly used in DBMS indexing.

4. SQL vs NoSQL

SQL uses structured schema. NoSQL is flexible (key-value, document, graph).
